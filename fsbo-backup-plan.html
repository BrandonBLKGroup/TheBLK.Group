The Reality of Selling FSBO

We respect the hustle. But here's what you're really signing up for.

📄

Legal Paperwork Complexity

Purchase agreements, disclosures, title work, inspections. One mistake could cost you thousands or kill the deal entirely. Are you confident you know every Arkansas disclosure requirement?

💰

Pricing Without Data

Zillow's estimate? Probably wrong. You don't have access to the same comparable sales data that realtors do. Overprice and you sit. Underprice and you leave money on the table.

📸

Marketing Costs Add Up

Professional photos? $200-500. Yard sign? $50. Online listings? Some are free, but premium placement costs money. Facebook ads? You're competing against professional marketers.

⏰

Time Investment Is Massive

Answering calls at all hours. Scheduling showings around YOUR life. Hosting open houses on weekends. Following up with every tire-kicker. Negotiating with buyers who know you're desperate.

🏠

Safety Concerns

You're letting strangers into your home. Do you know how to screen them? How to protect your family and your belongings? What if someone gets hurt on your property during a showing?

🔒

No MLS Access

80% of buyers work with agents who search the MLS. You're not on it. That means 80% of buyers will never see your home unless you pay a flat-fee MLS service (which still costs money).

🤝

Negotiating With Buyer's Agents

Buyer's agents know you're not a pro. They'll push you on price, terms, repairs. And they still want their 2.5-3% commission. Are you ready to negotiate against someone who does this for a living?

📋

Closing Coordination

Title company, lender, appraiser, inspector, buyer, buyer's agent. You're the quarterback coordinating all of it. Miss one deadline and the deal falls apart. Know what an estoppel letter is?

Still want to try? Go for it. But when you're ready for help, we're here.

Common Questions

Can I try selling FSBO first, then list with you later?

Absolutely. That's the whole point. Give it 30-60 days yourself. If it works, great. If not, we're here. No judgment. No "I told you so." Just results.

Why only 2% commission when most agents charge 5-6%?

Because we respect that you tried to save money by selling yourself. We're not here to punish you for that. 2% covers our marketing costs and keeps us profitable without gouging you.

What if I've already had a few showings but no offers?

Showings without offers usually mean one of two things: pricing or presentation. We'll help you figure out which it is and fix it. Sometimes it's as simple as better photos or staging.

Yes, if we list it. We'll put it on the MLS with professional marketing, which means more exposure than your FSBO listing anyway. But we move fast. Usually within 48 hours.

What if my house doesn't sell in 30 days?
